[Southwest Georgia Public Health District](http://www.southwestgeorgiapublichealth.org/) serves Baker, Calhoun, Colquitt, Dougherty, Early, Grady, Lee, Miller, Mitchell, Seminole, Terrell, Thomas, and Worth counties.
